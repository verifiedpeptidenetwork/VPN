🟡 TUDCA

Bile Acid Support During Rapid Weight Loss

TUDCA (Tauroursodeoxycholic acid) is a water-soluble bile acid that supports liver health, helps break down dietary fats, and promotes metabolic wellness. While not a magic weight loss pill, research suggests it may aid weight management by improving insulin sensitivity, reducing cellular stress, and supporting the gut microbiome.

⚠ Rapid weight loss — whether from GLP-1 medications, starvation/crash dieting, or any aggressive weight loss trend — attacks muscle tissue and can cause over-production of bile. This is when TUDCA support is most useful.

How TUDCA Interacts with Bile and Weight Loss

  • Fat Emulsification: As a bile salt, TUDCA helps break down fats in the small intestine, allowing the body to better utilize dietary fats and absorb fat-soluble vitamins (A, D, E, K).
  • Insulin Regulation: Obesity often triggers endoplasmic reticulum (ER) stress in cells, which can lead to insulin resistance. TUDCA acts as a chemical chaperone, relieving this stress, which in turn helps improve insulin sensitivity and the body's ability to clear excess insulin from the bloodstream.
  • Metabolism: Some research suggests that certain bile acids can influence the hypothalamus to promote feelings of fullness and suppress appetite.
  • Synergy with Ox Bile: Because TUDCA is primarily used to stimulate bile flow and protect the liver, it does not supply the full spectrum of digestive enzymes needed for heavy fat digestion. Many people pair it with Ox Bile supplements to comprehensively support fat malabsorption issues.

Dosage & Considerations

Dose: 500–1500mg daily, often divided into multiple intakes

  • Generally well-tolerated, though high doses can occasionally cause mild GI symptoms like diarrhea, nausea, or abdominal discomfort.
